About Jeanmarie Jackman

"I'm still evolving" [Wm. Hurt, The Big Chill, 1983]

I'm a human being, a work-in-progress, who happens, at present, to be employed as a Legal Assistant. For the moment, I am living on Long Island.

I became interested in Front-End Web Design and Development at General Assembly, as a way to review and refresh previously learned/forgotten web design skills and to progress forward learning new, more current techniques and tools. I am primarily interested in Game Design, and this is my "jumping off" point, as well as a means with which to self-promote.


My Mission

As I learn more about front-end, I hope to one day work as a User Experience Designer with Blizzard Entertainment because I'd like to work in a company which values collaboration, cooperation and community and whose games are evolutionary and ground-breaking.
